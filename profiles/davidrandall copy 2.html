David Randall

Whether their needs are limited like resolving an issue at work or sharpening up their CV, or more multifaceted like building up their freelancing career, David coaches clients through the thinking and doing processes needed to get the job done.

Trained with Rob Nathan’s Career Counselling Services in 2008, David practiced with Career Energy in Chancery Lane with private clients from legal, public sector, IT, banking and creative industries. David then moved onto work as careers consultant for a local training company before developing, with two colleagues, a group-based coaching approach ‘6 Steps from Preparation to Opportunity’.
